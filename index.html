About Me

Welcome to my portfolio- I'm Andrew Frost and this website is a showcase of projects that I've worked on.

I've always had an interest in video games, but didn't decide to pursue it as a career until a little later in life. I first started programming in late 2012 by following some online JavaScript tutorials, and I liked it enough to pursue a degree in programming. I began my studies at Texas A&M University - Corpus Christi in Fall of 2013 where I was first introduced to C++ and got to delve deeper into programming. I gained a lot of experience at college and would recommend it for most people interested in programming, but it's far from the only way to learn. I graduated Cum Laude and received a Bachelor of Science Degree in Game Programming in May of 2016.

Feel free to look around, browse the source code, and play the demos of anything that catches your eye!

If you're a recruiter in the Austin area looking for a strong C++ programmer, you can contact me by email at andrewdylanfrost@gmail.com.
